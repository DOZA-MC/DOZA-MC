## 1. Mandatory Verification
*   **Screening Process:** All members must complete a mandatory screening process via our verification system to gain access to the rest of the server.
*   **No Alternatives:** There are **no alternative methods** for bypassing this step. 
*   **Security:** This measure is strictly enforced to maintain high security standards and ensure the server remains a safe space for everyone.

## 2. Respect Everyone (Toxicity & Harassment)
Treat others with kindness and respect. Racism, sexism, homophobia, harassment, hate speech, and general toxicity are strictly prohibited.

## 3. No NSFW or Inappropriate Content
This server is for general audiences. NSFW, explicit, gore, or sexually suggestive content (including text and images) is banned.

## 4. Compliance, Privacy, and Communication
*   **Platform Compliance:** All members must adhere to Discord's [Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) and [Privacy Guidelines](https://discord.com/privacy). Any activity that violates Discord's policies is prohibited here.
*   **Language:** Please speak English in public channels to ensure clear communication and prevent misunderstandings.
*   **Standards:** Maintain a mature standard of communication; avoid excessive "brainrot" slang or inappropriate roleplay terms.

## 5. Privacy and Safety
*   **Do not** share personal information (yours or others), such as addresses, passwords, or real names.
*   **Do not** share private messages or DMs without explicit consent from all parties involved.
*   **Do not** engage in illegal activities or share pirated content.

## 6. Spam and Advertising
*   Do not spam messages, links, emojis, or mentions (including mass `@here`/`@everyone` pings).
*   Do not advertise external servers, products, or services without prior permission from the Administration Team.

## 7. Server Structure & Staff
*   **Channels:** Use channels for their intended purpose (e.g., keep discussions relevant to the channel topic).
*   **Staff Instructions:** Follow the instructions of the staff team. They are responsible for maintaining order.
*   **Bots:** Do not use unauthorized bots, user-bots, or automation tools.

## 8. Role Requests
*   Do not ask for ranks, admin positions, or special roles. Roles are assigned based on merit, effort, and necessity, not upon request.

## 9. Artist Role & AI Art Policy
*   **Obtaining the Role:** To obtain the **Artist** role, you must send **original art** in the `#media` channel.
*   **Patience:** Do not ask any staff member about the status of your application. Staff will review submissions in their own time.
*   **Inquiries:** If you have posted original art and have not received the role after **3 days**, you may politely ask a staff member about it.
*   **AI Art Policy:**
    *   **General Users:** Posting AI-generated content is prohibited, as it is not original work. Doing so will result in a **Warning**.
    *   **Artist Role Holders:** If you hold the Artist role and are found posting AI-generated content, your role will be **revoked immediately**, and you will be **permanently ineligible** to obtain the role again.

## 10. Account Conduct
*   **Identity:** Your username and avatar must be appropriate for a general audience.
*   **Impersonation:** Impersonation of staff, other members, or public figures is banned.
*   **Loopholes:** Do not attempt to bypass rules using technicalities or loopholes. The spirit of the rules is as important as their literal interpretation.

## 11. Moderation & Appeals
*   If you have a question regarding a moderation action (mute, ban, etc.) or an application, **open a ticket** in the designated channel.
*   Do not discuss bans, mutes, or application statuses in public chat or DM staff members about them.

### Punishment Guidelines

Failure to follow the rules will result in consequences based on the severity of the offense and the user's history.

#### Offense Tiers

**Tier 1: Minor Infractions**
*   **Definition:** Accidental rule breaks, minor spam, slight disrespect, or first-time low-level violations.
*   **Punishment:** **Verbal Warning** or **Timeout (up to 10 minutes)**.

**Tier 2: Moderate Infractions**
*   **Definition:** Intentional disruption, repeated spamming, mild toxicity, or ignoring staff instructions.
*   **Punishment:** **Formal Warning** or **Timeout (1 hour)**.

**Tier 3: Severe Infractions**
*   **Definition:** Harassment, serious toxicity, unauthorized advertising, posting AI content, or repeated Tier 2 offenses.
*   **Punishment:** **Softban** (Kick and message delete) or **Timed Ban (1-7 days)**.

**Tier 4: Critical Infractions**
*   **Definition:** Illegal activities, posting private information (doxxing), severe hate speech, NSFW content, or attempting to hack/raid the server.
*   **Punishment:** **Permanent Ban**.

#### Repeated Offenses & Escalation

We utilize a "Three Strike" system for escalating punishments. If a user continues to break rules after being punished, the consequences will become more severe.

*   **1st Offense:** Usually results in a **Warning** or **Timeout**.
*   **2nd Offense:** If the user repeats the offense or breaks a different rule shortly after, this will result in a **Severe Warning** or **Temporary Ban**.
*   **3rd Offense:** Continued disregard for the rules will result in a **Permanent Ban**.

**Note:** Staff reserve the right to skip tiers based on the severity of the situation. For example, a single severe violation (like posting illegal content) may result in an immediate Permanent Ban without prior warnings.
